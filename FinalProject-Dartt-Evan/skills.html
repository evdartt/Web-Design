About Me

Hi, I am Evan Dartt. I am currently a senior at Indiana University Bloomington completing a B.S. in Cybersecurity Informatics.


During my experience at IU, I have obtained a wide array of technical skills including knowledge in Python, HTML, mySQL, PHP, Java, C, and Unix. My area of study in cybersecurity has allowed me to become highly familiar with industry security standards and knowledgeable in data loss prevention. I am also working on completing my compTIA Security+ certification.


I am also the Esports Officer of Gaming@IU, one of the largest clubs at IU. I help set up events and tournaments for students, and communicate with team captains of our various competitive teams to plan events and coordinate matches to be streamed. I am also the President of the IU Collegiate Rocket League chapter, and General Manager of the Rocket League competitive teams at IU, where I coordinate matches and sign up teams for various competitions throughout the year.


I will be actively looking for internships and full-time job opportunities for the summer of 2022. If you have any questions feel free to contact me.
